=== Rumors & Legends ===
According to the Aurora's Whole Realms Catalogue , Elminster owed his longevity in part to a hidden cache of elven bread imported from Evermeet . [ 350 ] Others ascribed his many years to "more mundane" means, like drinking from potions of longevity , vitality , or the elixir of life . [ 51 ]
Some believed Elminster had a hand in the founding of the city of Waterdeep , or just with setting up its system of lords . [ 32 ]
